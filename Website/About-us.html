Terapanth Bhavan

Terapanth Bhavan is a spiritual and cultural center dedicated to promoting the teachings and principles of Terapanth, a Jain sect. It serves as a hub for community gatherings, religious ceremonies, and educational programs, fostering spiritual growth and social harmony among its members.

Shri Tulsi Mahapragya Foundation

The Shri Tulsi Mahapragya Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to spreading the wisdom and teachings of Acharya Tulsi and Acharya Mahapragya. Through various initiatives in education, health, and social welfare, the foundation strives to create a positive impact on society, embodying the principles of non-violence, compassion, and self-realization.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters spiritual growth, community engagement, and cultural preservation. We strive to be a beacon of Jain values and principles, offering resources and support for individuals on their spiritual journey.

Our Vision

To become a leading center for Jain philosophy and practice, inspiring individuals to lead ethical lives and contribute positively to society. We envision a world where the principles of non-violence, compassion, and spiritual awareness are widely embraced and practiced.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.

Compassion

We show empathy and kindness to everyone we serve.

Community

We believe in the power of community and work collaboratively to achieve our goals.
